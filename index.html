About

Our family owned campsite is located in the beautiful Kolpa valley, our cosy campsite offers a peaceful retreat from city life.
We provide a variety of affordable camping experiences, from tent sites, trailers to camper options.

Accomodation

We provide space, showers, water and electricity for all our guests. We also provide 3 shared fridges and free WiFi.

See our pricelist and calculate price of your visit.
